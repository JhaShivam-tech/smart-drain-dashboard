Problem: Mumbai Flooding Crisis

Every monsoon, waterlogging paralyzes Mumbai due to outdated drainage & sudden blockages. Over 70% of low-lying areas face severe flood risks. Manual inspection delays response & endangers lives.

Solution: IoT + Predictive AI

Network of ultrasonic flow sensors, moisture detectors & gas sensors stream live data. ML models predict blockages 48h in advance, enabling proactive cleaning & dynamic alert dispatch to BMC control rooms.
