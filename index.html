Smart Drainage Blockage Prediction

IoT & Sensor-driven Early Warning System for Urban Flood Mitigation

The Problem

During the monsoon, Mumbai faces severe waterlogging due to unmonitored plastic and silt accumulation in the underground drainage network. Reactive clearing is often too late, leading to traffic gridlocks, property damage, and health hazards.

The Solution

A proactive IoT sensor network deployed across critical junctions. By measuring water flow, gas pressure (methane buildup), and moisture in real-time, our predictive algorithm calculates blockage probability before flooding occurs.
